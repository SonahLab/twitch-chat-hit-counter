## Overview
As a Software Engineer, when things go wrong you need your system to tell you immediately when unexpected failures happen.

It's helpful to have a methodological, systematic approach to debugging real issues in PROD. Checking logs, checking dashboards to diagnose E2E where along the entire system the root cause can be found.

<br>
